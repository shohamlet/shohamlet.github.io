About me

I am a Royal Society University Research Fellow and a Lecturer at University College London. Before joining UCL, I was a University Research Fellow at the University of Cambridge for a short spell. Before that I was a Junior Fellow at the Institute for Theoretical Studies, ETH, Zürich, and a member of Prof. Benny Sudakov's group. I was also a Junior Research Fellow at Murray Edwards College, Cambridge. I completed my PhD in 2015 in Cambridge, under the supervision of Prof. Béla Bollobás. Before that, I did Part III in Cambridge, and received a Master's degree from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem under the supervision of Prof. Ehud Friedgut.

See also my CV (updated September 2020).

Research interests

My main interests are extremal and probabilistic combinatorics and Ramsey theory.
